Combat Round Structure p. 46

1. Draw initiative cards (1-10) — one per character. Lowest acts first.

2. Each turn: one free movement (10m) + one action.

3. After acting or reacting, flip card face-down — no further reactions allowed.

4. Wait: Swap initiative card with a later creature (they can't refuse). Monsters never wait.

5. Cards are redrawn every round.

Environmental Hazards p. 57-59

Cold
BUSHCRAFT roll per shift (per stretch/round in extreme cold). No blanket = bane, fur = boon. Fail = D6 HP + D6 WP, can't heal except by magic. 0 HP in cold = death at next roll.
Drowning
Half movement, no ranged, bane on melee. SWIMMING roll per stretch (per round in heavy armor). Underwater: CON each round or D6 dmg/round. 0 HP = death (only failed rolls count).
Falling
D6 per 2m fallen (rounded down). Under 2m = no damage. ACROBATICS halves dice (rounded up). Armor does NOT protect.
Fire
Torch as weapon = small wooden club + fire damage.

Encumbrance p. 32

Carry up to STR / 2 (rounded up) items in inventory.

Weapons at hand (up to 3) and worn armor/helmet don't count.

4 rations = 1 slot. Under 100 coins = free, 100-199 = 1 slot.

Over-encumbered: STR roll to move each round/shift. Fail = drop items or stay put.

Surprise & Sneak Attacks p. 48

Sneak Attack: SNEAKING roll to approach. Success = choose any initiative card, boon on attack, target can't dodge/parry. Subtle weapons double damage dice.

Ambush: Victims roll AWARENESS (bane if ambushers prepared). Fail = bottom initiative cards.

Mounted Combat p. 50

Mount must be combat trained. Move at mount's rate. Attacks/parries use lowest of RIDING and weapon skill.

RIDING replaces EVADE. No saddle = bane on all rolls. Ground melee vs mounted = bane.

Enemies choose: target rider or mount. Mount at 0 HP = dead (no death rolls).

NPC Attribute Guidelines p. 109

STR & AGL: Use damage bonus. +D6 = 17, +D4 = 14, none = 10.

CON: Roll against max HP, -2 per Robust level.

WIL: Roll against max WP (if listed), -2 per Focused level. If no WP listed, use 10.

INT & CHA: Roll against 10.
