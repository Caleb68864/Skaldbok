Helping

Round Rest
Recover D6 WP. Once per shift only.

Movement p. 43

Base
Move up to your Movement rate in meters per turn (free).
Dash
Use your action to double movement this round.
Leaping
Horizontal leap up to half movement with ACROBATICS. Quarter or less = no roll needed.
Door
Passing through a closed unlocked door costs half movement.
Enemies
Can't move past a standing enemy (~2x2m block). Must topple or reduce to 0 HP first. Friendlies pass freely.
Reaction
Performing a reaction (parry/dodge) also loses your movement for the round.

Boons, Banes & Pushing p. 42-43

Boon: Roll 2d20, keep the lower (better) result. Bane: Roll 2d20, keep the higher (worse) result. Multiple boons/banes cancel 1:1.

Pushing: After a failed skill/attribute roll, reroll (with boon/bane = reroll both dice). New result applies. You always suffer a condition of your choice (can't pick one you already have). Cannot push a Demon (20). All 6 conditions = no more pushing. Only PCs can push.

Damage Bonus: STR 13+ (or AGL for ranged/subtle) = +D4. STR 16+ = +D6.

Combat Round Structure p. 46

1. Draw initiative cards (1-10) — one per character. Lowest acts first.

2. Each turn: one free movement (10m) + one action.

3. After acting or reacting, flip card face-down — no further reactions allowed.

4. Wait: Swap initiative card with a later creature (they can't refuse). Monsters never wait.

5. Cards are redrawn every round.

Encumbrance p. 32

Carry up to STR / 2 (rounded up) items in inventory.

Weapons at hand (up to 3) and worn armor/helmet don't count.

4 rations = 1 slot. Under 100 coins = free, 100-199 = 1 slot.

Over-encumbered: STR roll to move each round/shift. Fail = drop items or stay put.

Environmental Hazards p. 57-59

Cold
BUSHCRAFT per shift (stretch/round extreme). No blanket = bane, fur = boon. Fail = D6 HP + D6 WP, no healing except magic. 0 HP = death next roll.

D6 per 2m (rounded down). Under 2m = none. ACROBATICS halves dice. Armor does NOT protect.
Drowning
Half move, no ranged, bane melee. SWIMMING per stretch (per round heavy armor). Underwater: CON/round or D6 dmg. 0 HP = death.

Torch as weapon = small wooden club + fire damage.

Death & Dying p. 55

At 0 HP: Make a CON death roll each round. 3 successes = recover with D6 HP. 3 failures = death.

Dragon on death roll = 2 successes. Demon = 2 failures. Taking damage at 0 HP = auto failed roll.

Instant death: Single attack reduces HP to negative of max HP.

After recovery: Roll CON. Failure = permanent injury (D10 table).

Sneak Attacks & Ambush p. 43, 48

Sneak Attack: SNEAKING roll (melee within 2m = bane). Success = choose any initiative card, boon on attack, can't dodge/parry. Subtle weapons +1 damage die. One attacker vs one target.

Ambush: Victims roll AWARENESS (bane if ambushers prepared). Fail = bottom initiative cards (#10+).

Mounted Combat p. 50

Mount must be combat trained. Move at mount's rate. Use lowest of RIDING and weapon skill.

RIDING replaces EVADE. No saddle = bane. Ground melee vs mounted = bane. Mount at 0 HP = dead (no death rolls).

NPC Attribute Guidelines p. 109

WIL: Roll against max WP (if listed), -2 per Focused level. If no WP listed, use 10.

INT & CHA: Roll against 10.
